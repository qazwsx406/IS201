I dedicate this slander page to... Korean Water Deers
So... Why Water Deers?
I know, I could have made this scratch page something more interesting, but I needed a place to vent out my hostility toward these creatures. They live mostly in Korea, and there are too many of them. They are extremely annoying. While I was serving in the military, one of these guys snuck into our tennis court, I went in the court and tapped into my inner hunter gatherer ancestral memories. We had a fun time together. Ah.. memories...


Fun Facts
  1. They are not the brightest
    • For real... they just stop in the middle of the highway and wonder why drivers hate them.
    • In Japan, they (apparantly) call someone an idiot by saying that they are a water deer.
  2. They are useless to humans
    • Water deer leather is too weak to do anything with it.
    • They are known for their unappetizing taste.
    • There are so many of these guys in Korea and they are wrecking farmlands.
